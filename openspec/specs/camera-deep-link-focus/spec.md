## Purpose

Define how camera deep links focus terrain when opened from map-compatible URLs versus exact viewer share URLs.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Surface-aware map deep link focus
The viewer SHALL treat URL camera states without an explicit exact-focus marker as map-compatible deep links and focus them on the best available visible surface at the supplied horizontal coordinates.

#### Scenario: Map link targets air above terrain
- **WHEN** the viewer starts from a URL with `x`, `y`, `z`, `zoom`, `theta`, and `phi` but no exact-focus marker, and the visible surface at `x,y` is materially below the supplied `z`
- **THEN** the camera target MUST be retargeted to the visible surface altitude while preserving the requested `zoom`, `theta`, and `phi`

#### Scenario: Surface is not available immediately
- **WHEN** the viewer starts from a map-compatible URL before relevant terrain or voxel geometry is loaded
- **THEN** the viewer MUST use a deterministic initial target and retry surface retargeting after relevant visible geometry becomes available

### Requirement: Exact viewer share restoration
The viewer SHALL mark URLs generated by the 3D viewer's share action as exact-focus links and SHALL restore exact-focus links without surface retargeting.

#### Scenario: Viewer share link is opened
- **WHEN** the viewer starts from a URL containing `x`, `y`, `z`, `zoom`, `theta`, `phi`, and the exact-focus marker generated by the share action
- **THEN** the camera target MUST use the supplied `x`, `y`, and `z` exactly and MUST restore the supplied `zoom`, `theta`, and `phi`

### Requirement: Surface retargeting preserves navigation usability
The viewer SHALL ensure camera zoom and orbit behavior after map-compatible retargeting operates around the corrected visible surface focus rather than the original supplied air point.

#### Scenario: User zooms after opening a map-compatible link
- **WHEN** a map-compatible link has been retargeted from a supplied altitude to a visible surface altitude
- **THEN** subsequent zoom and orbit interactions MUST operate around the corrected surface-level target
